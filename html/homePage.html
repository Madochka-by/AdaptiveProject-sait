Promoting a legal consulting website

How we extracted leads in the face of strict legal frameworks

The client's company provides businesses with comprehensive legal support services, including licensing in foreign jurisdictions, company registration, and opening bank accounts abroad. In short, you can contact them to register your business abroad, and they will assist you with all the necessary paperwork.

This project has been with us for almost 3 years!

Back in 2022, the client approached us with a simple and obvious request: to generate leads.

How was the site promoted before?

The client had already worked on SEO promotion. They left the previous team due to a lack of feedback and dissatisfaction with the results. That's why the customer immediately indicated that engagement and a quick response to queries were important to him. This wasn't an issue for us. We believe the project's growth is primarily the result of the SEO team's and the client's joint efforts.

The client requested promotion in the Russian market in two search engines:Google and Yandex. It was important to consider the requirements of both search engines. Google prioritizes content that is useful to users because the system's goal is to answer queries as accurately as possible. This is influenced by the quality of the text and the authority of the site, i.e., how many and what types of resources link to it.

Yandex is a commercial search engine. It focuses on behavioral factors and the clickability of a page's snippet.

What's special about the niche?

We can identify the promotion's peculiarities right away because of the niche. Before starting work, we determined who needs the company's services, who is competing for clients, and what difficulties we would face.

The main limitation in this case was the law prohibiting the promotion of certain services. For instance, we could only indirectly promote mining because it was banned in the Russian Federation until 2023. However, we quickly made up for it when the law changed.

Now, this sector is in the top 8!

Mining (mining) - production of cryptocurrency by increasing the capacity of computer equipment.

The project has no direct competitors, but many indirect ones. Most of them offer general legal services, while this project focuses on business and consulting.

After studying all the nuances, we developed a comprehensive promotional approach that coincides with our corporate philosophy. Our team looks for solutions to specific problems for specific clients. Each project has its own goals, target audience, and story.

What we improved as part of our integrated approach:

01
★

Semantics

Semantics was collected in iterations according to the client's priorities. For example, we started with the promotion of cryptocurrency licenses and legal support for MLM projects. We selected queries in these areas and studied the demand for these services simultaneously. First, specialists parsed all the semantics, and then they manually checked each query. After clustering the queries, they created a landing page for each group or chose one from existing ones.

While compiling the SN, we identified topics and services that had not been covered before and added the most relevant ones. This helped ensure client growth through new directions.

Our strategy focused on both high- and low-frequency queries to attract highly relevant traffic.

02
★

Content

We began the promotional process by determining the client's priority commercial pages. After analyzing the metrics, we found a problem with the bounce rate on the informational text, so we decided to reduce it. Because, when users quickly leave a page, it negatively impacts the site's position: search engines begin to consider it useless. To address this, we added more information about services and prices.

This way, our page will not be mistaken for an informational page. In parallel, we wrote articles for the blog. The articles are usually about starting a business in a particular region, details about obtaining licenses, and so on.

Technical audit

At this stage, we looked for technical errors on the site, checked the loading speed and vulnerability of our page. This is how we created a solid foundation for effective promotion. Here we checked 60+ parameters. We finalized the following:

  • we implemented the missing AggregateRating microformat. Now, the footer displays the company's rating on a five-point scale, as well as its overall rating on Google and Yandex Maps;
  • we increased the site's loading speed through caching and code optimization so users would not leave our page due to long loading times;
  • then, eliminated cyclic links to avoid indexing difficulties;
  • we also worked on the CNC URL and removed unnecessary quotes and brackets from the page addresses. This makes it easier for users to understand what our site is all about;
  • redirects are now direct, without intermediate redirects. A redirect chain that isn't necessary can make it harder to organize pages, which isn't good for the site;
  • and etc.
04
★

Usability

We analyzed the pages with high bounce rates and identified the website's weak points. For example, there were not enough capture forms, and the commercial pages had too much informational content. To improve user engagement and the depth of browsing, the team made the following edits:

  • The team added a unique selling point (USP), a search bar, and a request form to the header of the site. The header is the first thing users see when they visit the site. Adding the USP was important so that the company's proposal would be clear at first glance. Adding a search bar also made navigating the site much easier.

05
★

Linkbuilding

We actively work with links, building up two-level ones. At the start of the project, the site had 627 external links, while competitors averaged 4,922. Therefore, one of the most important tasks was to gradually approach the average value of competitors. We evenly distributed new links between the home page, services, and news, so that, in the eyes of search engines, the build-up would appear natural and not raise suspicion (shh!). We obtained links to sites that our target audience may be interested in, such as news portals and information resources about business and legal services. We also prioritized the domain zone — .ru was our preference.

What's the current status of the project?

Content remains a priority for this project. We are blogging on the site, writing articles on relevant topics, and building links. Overall, we are steadily completing the assigned tasks. There has been growth, even over the last three months. For all tracked queries, we are now in the top 21 on Yandex and in the top 28 on Google.
